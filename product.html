Shopify Conversion Track

Conversion tracking in Shopify is a feature that allows you to track how well your store is performing in terms of sales and conversions. This can be done by setting up conversion tracking in your Shopify admin, and then adding a conversion tracking code to your store's theme. By doing this, you'll be able to see how many people are visiting your store, how many of them are buying something, and what the average order value is.

GA4 Server Side Tracking

GA4 server-side tracking is a method of tracking user behavior on a website that involves sending data directly from the server to Google Analytics. Unlike client-side tracking, which relies on scripts running in the user's browser, server-side tracking occurs on the website's server.
